Asset Management Approaches

Typical asset management firms look for diversification across different regions and asset classes, focusing on factor exposures such as value, quality, growth, and momentum to harvest risk premia in multi-asset portfolios. Sometimes their portfolios incorporate sophisticated top-down engines for portfolio allocation and performance analytics.

We plan to explore or scrape some preliminary data on these underlying assets to form a purely hypothetical risk scenario.

Financial Dashboard Features

Asset-Allocation Slider & Mean-Variance Engine

  • Weight Adjustment: sliders for Equity, Fixed Income, and Alternatives.
  • Automated Calculation: portfolio mean and variance updated in real time.
  • Efficient Frontier: scatter plot of optimized portfolios via quadratic optimization.

Risk-Return Distribution Visualizer

  • Monte Carlo Simulation: 1,000 paths based on historical return statistics.
  • Density Curves: overlayed return distributions for each allocation.

Performance Metrics & Ratios

  • Key Indicators: annualized return, volatility, Sharpe ratio, maximum drawdown.
  • Risk Measures: Value-at-Risk (VaR) and Conditional VaR at user-selected confidence levels.

Factor Attribution & Scenario Analysis

  • Factor Attribution: break down returns by market, size, value, momentum via regression.
  • Predefined Scenarios: e.g., 2008 equity crash or 2020 bond rally, and their impact on portfolio P&L.

Dashboard UI

  • Rapid Prototyping: build with Streamlit or Dash.
  • Interactive Charts: use Plotly for efficient frontier, density plots, and factor bar charts.
  • Data Processing: pandas and NumPy for data handling and simulation math.
